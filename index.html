Marcelo Somers

I believe computers empower people to do things they never could. Designing for simplicity and intuitiveness makes that power accessible to more people. I help clients design tools to solve real problems at Slalom Consulting in Dallas, TX. My technical emphasis is on Design Systems and CSS Architecture. I also lead our UI prototyping team that focuses on helping companies visualize their solutions in appropriate mediums.

I am a regular speaker on topics related to design and development. I co-created and maintain PatternPack, an open source tool for designing, developing, and distributing design systems.
