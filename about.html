About

Marcelo Somers is a designer and developer focused on Design Systems, Prototyping, and CSS Architecture. He leads a team of UI Developers at slalom, where we help clients design and build software that empowers users and solves their pains.

Marcelo co-created PatternPack, an open-source tool for designing, documenting, and distributing Pattern Libraries. He is a regular speaker, focusing on helping design and development teams work better together.

This site was created with Jekyll, deployed with Codeship, and hosted on Digital Ocean. You can view the source on GitHub.
